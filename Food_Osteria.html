Osteria Savio Volpe is a popular Italian restaurant located in Vancouver, Canada. Here is some information you can use on your website: Concept: Osteria Savio Volpe is a warm and inviting Italian restaurant that serves rustic dishes made with fresh, locally-sourced ingredients. The restaurant's name translates to "Savio the Fox's Tavern," and the atmosphere is meant to evoke the feeling of a traditional Italian osteria. Menu: The menu at Osteria Savio Volpe features a variety of Italian dishes, including antipasti, pasta, pizza, and meat and fish entrees. Some of their signature dishes include the spaghetti alla chitarra, grilled octopus, and roasted chicken for two. The restaurant also has an extensive wine list, with many Italian varietals to choose from.
